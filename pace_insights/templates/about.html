I put this website together to help people who are looking to compare the different types of car finance in the UK.

It's perhaps purposely difficult to be able to compare the True financial costs of each type of deal.

When I was comparing financing options for my new car I ended up creating an Excel spreadsheet to help. I've now converted that spreadsheet into this website so that others can hopefully get a better idea of the True costs of each deal they've been offered.
